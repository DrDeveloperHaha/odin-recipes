World's Best Lasagna

Dish Description

This lasagna is widely considered the gold standard of comfort food, boasting over 20,000 five-star reviews for its perfectly balanced, meaty depth. The secret lies in the slow-simmered ragu, which combines sweet Italian sausage and lean ground beef with crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, and a hint of sugar for a sauce that is rich, thick, and savory. Layered between tender lasagna noodles is a creamy blend of ricotta cheese, egg, and fresh parsley, creating a velvety contrast to the robust meat and the gooey blankets of melted mozzarella and parmesan cheese.
Whether you are hosting a large family gathering or prepping a meal to freeze for later, this recipe delivers a hearty, multi-dimensional experience that tastes even better the next day. Each slice holds its structure beautifully, revealing the careful distribution of cheese and seasoned meat that has made it a legendary staple in kitchens across the world. It is a high-reward dish that transforms simple, classic ingredients into a decadent centerpiece that feels both nostalgic and impressively gourmet.

  • Meat:This super meaty lasagna has sweet Italian sausage and lean ground beef.
  • Onion and Garlic:An onion and two cloves of garlic are cooked with the meat to add tons of flavor.
  • Tomato Products:You'll need a can of crushed tomatoes, two cans of tomato sauce, and two cans of tomato paste.
  • Sugar:Two tablespoons of white sugar add subtle sweetness and enhance the flavor of the sauce.
  • Spices and Seasonings:This lasagna recipe is flavored with fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper.
  • Lasagna Noodles:Use store-bought or homemade lasagna noodles.
  • Cheeses:Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheese make this lasagna extra decadent.
  • Egg:An egg helps bind the ricotta so it doesn't ooze out of the lasagna when you cut into it.
